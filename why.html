Why do debating?

It's Fun!

No really, it's actually fun.

Learning

Not only will you learn about many various topics, but you'll also develop very transferable skills such as public speaking, developing arguments and thinking on your feet. If you think you're already pretty confident with speaking, well that's great, debating is another way to test yourself and learn more.

Friends

Of course you can and should make a team with your friends, and this is a great extra way to work together with them and learn and have fun. You may even make new friends who share common interests.

Competition

Every few weeks there is a round of debating against high school teams around Hamilton (and a little further away). As these rounds progress, the best teams will be put into quarter-finals, semi-finals, and final-finals. There isn't really a prize for winning this competition, but hey, it's fun and you get bragging rights.

There is also the Waikato Russel McVeagh debating competition, which we have recently done very well in. From this competition, there are various opportunity to move onto further national and international competitions.

Every year a senior and junior debating team from HBHS are sent to compete in the Super 8 competition against 7 other boys schools from around the North Island. Doing well in this helps the school in the competition and gives a chance to travel a bit away from school for a couple of days.

Service Opportunities

Experienced senior debaters have the opportunity to do adjudication for junior debates. As well as helping out and gaining service hours, you will also assuredly learn a lot from this to help you further with debating.
